LOCATION

In the Clearing Between
a Tranquil Lake and Evergreen Plains.

Menoke by the Lake is tucked away on a secluded bay overlooking Lake Couchiching in Orilla, Ontario, better known as “Sunshine City.”
The community is at the same time exceptionally private and intricately connected, being just steps from Menoke Beach and a 1 hour drive from Toronto. Orilla itself is a marvelous experience, with waterfalls, annual festivals and numerous cultural attractions making it a hub of tourism and opportunity—exploration and discovery are around every corner.

LAKESIDE LIVING

A Resort Community of
Sophisticated, Modern Homes

A four-seasons resort community, Menoke by the Lake is a collection of homes that are an alchemy of innovation and comfort, both design forward and welcoming. From gourmet kitchens and artistic living and dining rooms, to spacious bedrooms and spa-inspired bathrooms, each feature and finish has been meticulously considered to instill wonder. Nature stretches out before you. Private gardens and untouched trails lead to the expanse of Lake Couchiching and Menoke Beach, both just steps away.

REGISTER
RECREATION CENTRE

Leads You to a 10-Acre,
State-of-the-Art Recreation Centre

The sprawling 10-acre Westshore Recreation Facility, approved by the township of Severn, is to have a double gymnasium, two program rooms, a community room, catering kitchen, and outdoor spaces including multi-purpose sports fields and tennis courts, bocce ball courts and a playground. Several winding trails and transportation routes will lead to and from the facility, integrating it within the very heart of Menoke by the Lake.

* This centre will be funded, constructed and operated by the township of Severn based on the time schedule approved by the township. Facilities of the centre are listed using township website.
