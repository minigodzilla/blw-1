What is the payment schedule in terms of what percentage I need to pay up front and throughout the construction cycle until the move-in date?

As a standard practice in the industry, we require 5% of the cost of our home to be paid up front once you have signed the purchase agreement. Another 5% will be due 6 months later. An additional 5% will be due 1.5 years after your initial payment. Finally, the last 5% will be paid on the closing date. In total 20% of the cost of the home will be due by the closing date.
