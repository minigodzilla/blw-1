FUTURE DEVELOPMENT

Lake Couchiching Residence

Lake Couchiching Residence is located east of highway 11 (Yonge St.) and north of lake Couchiching. West and east side of the land are already developed residential areas.

The area of the land is 92 acres (37 hectare). Around 42 percent of the land will be developed and the rest will be kept untouched to be used as park with walking trails.

Based on the approved draft plan of the subdivision, the development includes 188 freehold residential units plus one management and security unit:

  • 8 blocks of 6 unit townhouses
  • 15 blocks of 2 unit semi-detached houses
  • 110 Detached houses
  • 1 Management and security unit
