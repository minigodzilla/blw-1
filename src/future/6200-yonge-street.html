Belleville is a city located at the mouth of the Moira River on the Bay of Quinte in Southern (Central) Ontario, Canada, along the Quebec City-Windsor Corridor. It is the seat of Hastings County, but politically independent of it, and is the centre of the Bay of Quinte Region.

In 2016, the enumerated population of Belleville (City) was 50,716, which represents an increase of 2.6% from 2011. The land area of Belleville (City) is 247.25 square kilometres and the population density was 205.1 people per square kilometre. In 2016, there were 21,732 private dwellings occupied in Belleville (City), which represent an increase of 3.2% from 2011.
