FUTURE DEVELOPMENT

Hawk Ridge

The residences at Hawk Ridge are one part of an ambitious project that is to reimagine its residents’ lifestyles in Orillia. A draft plan has been approved for 368 detached homes and townhouses set on the 89 acres. The entire site, however, sprawls across 422 acres and includes a golf course, clubhouse and commercial development.

Hawk Ridge Golf & Country Club

The Hawk Ridge Golf and Country Club is a 45-hole public golf facility with two 18-hole Championship Courses (Meadow Nest and Timber Ridge), and a 9-hole Executive Par 3 Course (Hawk’s Island).

The Clubhouse

The heart of Hawk Ridge, a 15,000 square foot clubhouse, elegantly appointment and able to transform itself to host any occasion. It is home to over 160 annual events such as weddings, banquets and tournaments.

Commercial Development

Tucked away on 10 acres beside Highway 11, a commercial development will be raised as part of the project, servicing the community and immediate trade area.
