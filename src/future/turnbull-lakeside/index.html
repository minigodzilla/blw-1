FUTURE DEVELOPMENT

Turnbull Lakeside

Turnbull Lakeside sits on 92 acres of land east of Highway 11 and north of Lake Couchiching. Approximately 42% of the land will be developed, leaving the majority of it untouched and preserved as parkland with walking trails.

Based on the approved draft plan, Turnbull Lakeside will feature 188 freehold residences plus a management and security unit. Currently, the west and east sides of the site are already developed residential areas.

  • Eight Blocks of Six Unit Townhouses.
  • 15 Blocks of Two Unit Semi-Detached Houses.
  • 110 Detached Houses.
  • One Management and Security Unit.
