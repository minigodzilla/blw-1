FUTURE DEVELOPMENT

6200 Yonge Street

Yonge Street North is evolving from a largely commercialized area into a more diverse neighbourhood, both in form and fabric. As of today, there are three visions for 6200 Yonge, a residential community that is a marker of the change being undergone in the neighbourhood. The number of storeys and square footage to best serve the growing population and its demands are still under consideration.

Yonge Subway Extension

The Yonge Subway Extension is York Region’s highest transit priority and has been identified as the next wave project by Metrolinx. With walking access to both proposed Steeles and Cummer stations, 6200 Yonge is one of the area’s future developments that has been planned in line with the transforming fabric of the neighbourhood.
