FUTURE DEVELOPMENT

Memorial Avenue

Orillia is in the midst of rapid development. The city has only three points available for residential development, one of which is Memorial Avenue. The community is to feature 486 units across 45 acres of land, with an additional 170 units proposed once rezoning is finalized.

Plans are also in place for Memorial Avenue to hold commercial units in the south-east corner of the site, which will serve its residents and the wider community.
